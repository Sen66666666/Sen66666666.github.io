    FIVE-BAR PLANAR PARALLEL ROBOT

    The five-bar planar parallel robot is a closed-loop structure composed of five links. The whole system has two degrees of freedom, and its range of motion is only in the two-dimensional plane. Before writing the motion program of the 5-bar parallel robot, I need to derive the forward and inverse kinematics formulas first, and use MATLAB to draw the forward and inverse motion trajectories of the robot. Then write code to connect the robot to the Arduino board to control the robot.
